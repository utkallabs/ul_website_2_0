Al/ML solution

AI/ML, or Artificial Intelligence/Machine Learning, refers to the use of advanced algorithms and statistical models to enable computer systems to perform tasks and make decisions without explicit programming. It involves techniques that allow machines to learn from data, recognize patterns, and improve performance over time, driving automation, prediction, and intelligent decision-making.
